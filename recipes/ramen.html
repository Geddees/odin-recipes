Poggers Ramen

Description

What do you think is the best dish in the world? That's right, it's ramen! Don't know what you should eat? Ramen. Hung over? Ramen. Easy decision 100% of the time.

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles
  • Broth (Miso or Mizu)
  • Chasu
  • Bamboo shoots

Steps

  1. Put your noodles in a bowl
  2. Add the chasu in an asthetic manner to the noodles.
  3. Pour the broth into the bowl
  4. Allow the broth to soften the noodles
  5. Hit it with a dash of bamboo shoots
  6. Enjoy!
