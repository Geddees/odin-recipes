Pizza without Pineapple

Description

The first thing is to recognize that pineapple is gross. After this, you will need to determine that basically any pizza is amazing.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup water
  • pinch of salt
  • cheese to your liking
  • some basil

Steps

  1. Mix the flour and the water together
  2. Add the salt
  3. Bake in oven at 350 degrees for 10 minutes
  4. Add cheese after 2 minutes
  5. When done, hit it with the basil and enjoy!
